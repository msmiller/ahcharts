http://gonzoville.com/ahcharts/index.php

Pick 4 planes from the list on the left. Hit submit, it loads up the comparisons.

Gives you Mil speed chart, Wep speed chart. Mil climb rates, wep climb rates.
Acceleration, turn radius (with and without full flaps) Lethality, and fireing time.

This is an awesome tool, really lets you get a feel for what a plane is capable of before ever leaving the ground.

Take a good look at the F4u's turn rate, some suprises there with full flaps.
